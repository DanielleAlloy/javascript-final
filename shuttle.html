Bring your Wallet

Don't forget, a valid Farmingdale ID is required to board the shuttle! The driver may ask you to present your Farmingdale ID before boarding. Need to get an ID or replacement, contact the University Police to get a new ID.

Luggage & Large Carry-ons

Luggage, large bags and small motility devices are allowed on the shuttle and should be placed in the rear of the bus. Students are responsible for their own personal belongings - the driver does not load or unload baggage. The shuttles are also wheelchair accessible.

Backup Plan

What are your options if you miss the shuttle? Here are some fares from local companies:

  • A taxi from Farmingdale College Laffin Hall to the Farmingdale LIRR is approximately $10.88. A taxi from the Farmingdale LIRR to the Aviation Center is approximately $10.62. You can contact LI Yellow Cab via phone at 516-249-1212.
  • An Uber from Farmingdale College Laffin Hall to the Farmingdale LIRR is approximately $7. An Uber from the Farmingdale LIRR to the Aviation Center is approximately $8. Fares are variable and can increase depending on demand. You can book an Uber on the mobile app, which is available on Android and iOS platforms.
  • A Lyft from Farmingdale College Laffin Hall to the Farmingdale LIRR is approximately $7.82. A Lyft from the Farmingdale LIRR to the Aviation Center is approximately $8.32. Fares are variable and can increase depending on demand. Lyft does frequently offer specials for 50% off fares. You can book a Lyft on the mobile app, which is available on Android and iOS platforms.

FAQ

How much does the shuttle cost?
The shuttle is free for all all students, faculty, staff, visitors, and alumni.
Does the shuttle run year round?
No, the shuttle only operates during the Fall and Spring semesters. It starts running on the first day of class, and continues through finals week. It only operates Monday-Friday.
How does the shuttle remain operational? Who pays for it?
The shuttle is funded by the Student Transportation Fee, Auxiliary Service Corporation (ASC), and Student Government Association (SGA).
