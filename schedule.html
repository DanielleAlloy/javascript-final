Farmingdale Campus Laffin Hall Shuttle Schedule

The shuttle starts running at 7:00 at the Farmingdale LIRR, making stops at Laffin Hall at the Farmingdale campus. It runs through till a 12:00-1:00PM lunch break. The last shuttle drop off of the day is at the Farmingdale LIRR at 11:50PM.
